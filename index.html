Common questions

What settings should I use?

Balanced is great for most uploads (email, forms, social). Use Smaller for the tiniest files. Use Lossless-ish when you need the original resolution with mild reduction.

Will it work with 4K videos?

Yes, but browser compression can be slow for long 4K clips. For best results, start with short clips or use the 720p preset.

Why no upload?

It’s faster and private. Everything runs in your browser.
