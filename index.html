Common questions

What settings should I use?

Balanced works great for most uploads (email, forms, social). Use Smaller for the tiniest files, or Lossless-ish for highest quality.

Will it work with 4K or large photos?

Yes — browser compression handles both, though long 4K videos or giant photos can take a few seconds.

Why no upload?

It’s faster and private. Everything runs entirely on your device.
